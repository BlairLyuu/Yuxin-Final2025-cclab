Biography

Yuxin Liu 刘钰昕

2006.07

Emerging designer and curator currently pursuing a BFA in Design and Technology with a minor in Curatorial Studies at Parsons School of Design in New York. My practice sits at the intersection of digital media, visual storytelling, and exhibition-making, with a particular interest in how art, technology, and narrative shape audience experience.

I have hands-on gallery experience in both the U.S. and China, working across exhibition production, visitor engagement, artist-facing coordination, and social media strategy. In parallel, I have contributed to independent film production, developing skills in proposal writing, fundraising support, and cross-disciplinary project management.

Key skills and competencies:
  • Exhibition planning, curatorial research, and narrative-driven spatial design
  • Visual communication: Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Premiere Pro, After Effects
  • Creative technology: TouchDesigner, Blender; familiarity with AI-assisted image and video tools (e.g., Stable Diffusion, Kaiber AI)
  • Content strategy and branding for Xiaohongshu and Instagram
  • Bilingual (Mandarin / English) communication, cross-cultural collaboration, and stakeholder coordination
